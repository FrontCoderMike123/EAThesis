Technologies

When it comes to building EasyEats, a lot of research goes into it. But there are some major tools involved in this creation. Below are the languages & programs that I used to develope this app.

Tools

HTML5 is used for the "Structure" of the house. This is used the build the outside of the app, the frame.

CSS3 is used to "Design" the house. The inside and outs of Easy Eats. The colors, style, template etc.

Javascript is used for the "Plumbing" of the house. Flic a light switch, on comes the power. You register a user, users created.

MongoDB is where the users information is stored. This allows the application to apply a login / register form and hold information.

ExpressJS is used to carry "Variables" over easily from function to function. This allows for personal "Shout Outs" to the user.

AngularJS is used to "Loop" through content with ease. Read JSON files and extract the needed information.

NodeJS is the "Back-Bone" of the opperation. Everything is compiled in NodeJs, routes, views, partials etc.
