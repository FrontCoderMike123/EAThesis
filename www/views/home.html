What is Easy Eats?

Easy Eats is a food finding app. It takes your entered budget along with your location & it returns to you results of restaurants in your area. Those restaurants will match your budget and ONLY show you food in which you can afford.

How It Works?

Example If you've entered $10.00, Easy Eats will only show you results of where you CAN afford to eat. It's quick, & it's personal. Easy Eats has a 'Cravings' option where you can choose to filter your searches with finer results. You're craving some Italian food? Let Easy Eats know!

Where To Find It?

Easy Eats is a responsive website. No matter which device you are using, from mobile phones to tablets, Easy Eats will ALWAYS be at your fingertips, and be there for you when you are feeling hungry.
