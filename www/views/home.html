What is Easy Eats?

Easy Eats is a food finding app, where I take your entered budget along with your location & I give you results of restaurants in your area. Those restaurants will match your budget and ONLY show you food in which you can afford.

How It Works?

Example: If you've entered $10.00, Easy Eats will only show you results where you CAN afford to eat. It's quick, & it's personal. Easy Eats has a 'Cravings' option where you can choose to filter your searches with finer results. You're hungry for a sub, Easy Eats will show you where to get a delicious sub.

Where To Find Easy Eats?

Easy Eats is a responsive website. From mobile to desktop, no matter where you may be, Easy Eats will always be at your finger tips.
